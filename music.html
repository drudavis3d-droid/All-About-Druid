Introduction

In this page you will see everything you need to know about Druid's music journey.

How it started

It all started from my very first music skill, which is singing. As it was the catalyst in my music journey at such a young age, over time when I was around 10 my parents introduced me to my first instrument which was piano.

At that time my interest in music lowered as I just disliked it as a kid for the sole reason being it just wasn't fun at all, especially when it came to piano.

The continuation

My interest in music started to develop again once I started grade 9 in Richmindale, as I joined a band (The Aces) with some of my friends.

At this point in my journey I started to find music enjoyable overall as I learned that music doesn't need to be serious, but it can be carefree and relaxing.

The Aces to Rinesa

As months went by, me and my friends started to feel "burnt out" and we noticed that the commitment we had for the band wasn't the same from when we first started, so we decided to disband.

I was pretty sad at this point because I really did care for the band, although I knew deep down it was eventually going to happen.

Surprisingly, a few weeks later, we decided to do band again. I was shocked because I genuinely thought that was the end of our band’s journey, yet I felt joyous and excited at the same time.

So this is how it went: all of us as a collective group decided for some unknown reason to do band again but in a different stage name called Rinesa.

In that moment I realized something important — a component we've been missing since grade 9: passion. Passion was the final piece of the puzzle that we didn’t know we were solving. Passion was the unknown variable that started Rinesa.

The Journey of Rinesa

The journey of rinesa truly began at grade 10, as at that time everything happened in Richmindale. we performed in events around the year such as Talent Show and although we didn't perfrom in prom we still managed to give our all in practices leading to that event.

Eventually our year in Grade 10 had ended so we couldn't really rely on the school to perform, so we decided to find gigs to perform and share our passion to people. Rinesa did find gigs to perform, twice in Burjuman and once in night market for battle of the bands sponsered under Eraserheads.

That's the journey of Rinesa so far, The story hasn't ended yet but its still in writing.

The Side Quest

Moving to the present day, although rinesa isn't as active as before, I still manage to find ways to progress as an artist under the stage name of aeon. I chose aeon as my stage name for the sole reason being it represents "Forever" whether thats covering or composing songs for decades, Music will always feel fun for me for a life time.

I guess the true reason why I chose aeon was all for my brother.
